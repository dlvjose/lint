Martyr!
9/10

This one is going to stick with me for a while. What an incredible story with such a well put together narrative. You're reading to parallel stories without ver realizing it. Cyrus' story and Roya/Orikideh's story. I interpreted the ending as Cyrus finally allowing himself to feel grace. Grace that he had so deeply lost and kept himself guarded from.
