The Drama
7/10
Went into this one completely in the dark and I'm glad I did. I knew a little of the twist based on what Kath had told me but I would've never in a thousand years expected what it actually was. That being said what is with this trend of making everyone an asshole in movies? Not saying it's a bad thing because it's certainly realistic but it has taken me by surprise but I guess I shouldn't expect anything else from an A24 film. I had the same feeling watching Lurker. Loved Zendaya's and Robert Pattinson's acting. Incredible way to put America's current reality on display.
